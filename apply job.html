Admission Requirements

For New Students

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts from previous school
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID
  • Medical certificate of good health
  • Recommendation letter from previous school (if applicable)

For Transfer Students

  • All requirements for new students
  • Official transcript from current school
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Record of completed coursework
  • Disciplinary record (if applicable)

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Kageyo TVET School offers various scholarship opportunities to support talented and deserving students. Our financial aid programs are designed to ensure that no qualified student is denied education due to financial constraints.

Available Scholarships

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship: For students with outstanding academic records
  • Need-Based Financial Aid: For students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds
  • Sports and Arts Scholarship: For students with exceptional talent in sports or arts
  • Community Leadership Scholarship: For students who have demonstrated leadership in their communities

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must meet the admission requirements of Kageyo TVET School
  • Must demonstrate financial need (for need-based scholarships)
  • Must maintain satisfactory academic performance throughout the program
  • Must adhere to the school's code of conduct

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the minimum requirements for admission?
The minimum requirements vary by program but generally include completion of O'Level education with satisfactory grades in relevant subjects. Please refer to the specific program requirements in our admission guidelines.
Is there an application fee?
Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of 5,000 RWF for all applicants. This fee covers the cost of processing your application.
Can I apply for multiple programs?
Applicants may indicate up to two program preferences on their application form. However, you will only be admitted to one program based on availability and your qualifications.
How will I know if my application has been received?
After submitting your online application, you will receive an automatic confirmation email. For offline applications, you will receive a confirmation via SMS or email within 3-5 business days.
What is the duration of the programs?
Most TVET programs at Kageyo TVET School are 2-3 years in duration, depending on the specific trade and certification level.
Are there accommodation facilities for students?
Yes, we have limited accommodation facilities on campus. Priority is given to students from distant locations. Please indicate your accommodation needs in your application.
